What We Cover

Qualifications crafted for the building services engineering sector

Building services engineering qualifications underpin the technical standards required to work safely and competently across domestic and commercial heating, gas, electrical, plumbing, and renewable energy systems. From initial entry-level awards to advanced professional certification, these qualifications ensure those in the trades meet the regulatory requirements of their sector.

LCL Awards develops and awards qualifications that are aligned with industry standards, employer expectations, and regulatory requirements. We cover the full breadth of the building services sector – from those new to the industry to those looking to upskill, requalify, or expand their provision, LCL Awards offers the right qualification pathways to help you progress.

All qualifications are delivered exclusively through LCL Awards' network of approved training centres, ensuring national consistency and local access to training.

Accreditation & Regulation

Trusted. Regulated. Recognised.

LCL Awards is accredited by UKAS (The United Kingdom Accreditation Service) under accreditation number 0230, against the internationally recognised standard BS EN ISO/IEC 17024:2012 for conformity assessment bodies operating certification of persons. This accreditation confirms that LCL Awards operates to the highest standards of impartiality, competence and consistent service delivery.

In addition to UKAS accreditation, LCL Awards qualifications are regulated by Ofqual in England, Qualifications Wales in Wales, and CCEA Regulation in Northern Ireland. This ensures that all LCL Awards qualifications sit on the national qualifications frameworks and carry the same regulatory weight as qualifications from any other regulated awarding organisation.

For learners, this means qualifications that are genuinely portable and recognised by employers, industry bodies and licensing schemes across the UK. For training centres, it means operating within a quality assurance framework that is transparent, consistent and independently verified.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about LCL Awards qualifications

What is an LCL Awards qualification?

LCL Awards qualifications are nationally recognised certificates awarded to individuals who have demonstrated competence in a specific building services engineering skill or trade, assessed and certified by LCL Awards as a regulated awarding organisation.

Are LCL Awards qualifications recognised by employers and industry bodies?

Yes. LCL Awards is regulated by Ofqual, Qualifications Wales and CCEA Regulation, and UKAS accredited. Qualifications are recognised by Gas Safe Register, MCS, Part P schemes and other relevant industry licensing bodies.

How do I take an LCL Awards qualification?

All LCL Awards qualifications are delivered through our network of approved training centres. You enrol and undertake training directly with a centre, which then submits your assessment results to LCL Awards for certification.

Where can I find a training centre near me?

Use the LCL Awards approved centre finder to search by location and sector. With over 160 approved centres across England, Wales and Northern Ireland, you can find training close to where you live or work.

What industries do LCL Awards qualifications cover?

LCL Awards covers the full building services engineering sector including gas (domestic and commercial), electrical installation, plumbing, heating, renewable energy, refrigeration, F-Gas, smart metering, oil firing, solid fuel and biomass.

How can my organisation become an approved LCL Awards training centre?

Training providers can apply to become an LCL Awards approved centre by contacting our centre approval team. You'll be guided through the approval process, quality assurance requirements and the support available to deliver our qualifications.
